Welcome!

C. Brandon Ogbunu is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Ecology and Evolutionary Biology at Brown University. He is an evolutionary systems biologist, and uses experimental evolution, mathematical modeling, and computational biology to better understand how adaptive evolution occurs in nature, mainly in microbial models of disease. In doing so, he aims to develop theory that enriches our understanding of the evolutionary process and contributes to practical solutions for clinical medicine and public health.
